Motivation Factor Towards Online Education
1.Method
Using online method is the new way to learn and teaching in this century. The teachers or lectures now can use online methods with many different Internet methods such as Whatsapp, Telegram, You Tube, Google Classroom and many more. Today, all world now in lockdown because of Covid-19 that spread to the almost all country in the world and one of the ways to prevent the spread of Covid-19 is avoid crowds. All schools and university are now close and this is the best way to learn and teaching using online methods.

2.Create a timetable
Keeping your goals realistic and attainable requires a sensible schedule. It might help to make a list of everything you have going on, from online courses and work to family obligations and social time. Online instructor Jarin Eisenberg suggest creating a master schedule for every two months and setting artificial deadlines for your schoolwork. Prioritizing your work by ranking everything from very important to not important, is also helpful. Remember that online courses allow you to set your own schedule, so avoid the out of sight, out of mind situation by logging in on a regular basis.

3.Stay positive
Feeling overwhelmed or getting behind can cause stress which is seriously demotivating. While its not easy to turn a negative attitude into a positive outlook, it will definitely help you keep going when you want to procrastinate on an assignment or dont feel like logging in to attend a lecture. If you try to stay positive throughout your online course experience, you will find it makes everything a little easier.

4.Reward for ourself
Accomplishing a goal or completing a project feels great, that alone is rewarding, but doing something nice for yourself can create even more internal motivation. As Eisenberg advises, celebrate the little achievements. For example, if you reach your weekly goal, you might treat yourself to brunch over the weekend. What makes you happy? Give it to yourself to acknowledge your accomplishments.

Here is a quote from The Posivity Blog

Anyone who has never made a mistake has never tried anything new. Albert Einstein
The only person who is educated is the one who has learned how to learn and change. Carl Rogers
The quote is written by Henrik Edberg in 2006
